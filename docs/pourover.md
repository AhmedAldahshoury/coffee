## Julian's Pourover Coffee
[James Hoffmann Video Recipe](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AI4ynXzkSQo)

1. prepare the coffee grinder
    1. crank the twisty thingy CW until stop (be gentle)
    2. crank it CCW for 22 clicks (pourover uses a coarse grind)
2. grind the beans
    1. grind 20g beans
    2. grind 10g beans (so the input does not clogg)
3. boil ca 700 ml of water
4. prepare the glassware
    1. add paper to funnel
    2. pour several 100g of hot water through filter (wets the paper, heats the glass)
    3. add ground coffee
        1. make a flat surface
        2. make a little pit with your finger for better pouring
5. brew by increasing volume to the given amount until the given time:

| H2O [g] | time | comments |
|:------|-----:| --------:|
| 60-80 | 0:45 | blooming, for CO2 extraction |
|   300 | 1:15 | try to keep surface even |
|   500 | 1:45 | swirl to flatten out surface |
|  wait | 3:30 | |
